# Brand & advertising visuals

Brand spirit, luxury perfume and jewelry, and automotive — expression-led ad concepts where the *feeling* carries the brand, not a spec sheet. (Vibe Creating is **partially** suited here: rewrite is optional, and user constraints like logos and slogans take priority.)

## Brand spirit / avant-garde concept

### A sportswear manifesto

> A sneaker brand film. A girl camps alone at a snow-mountain base camp; a "date with the mountain" timed reminder pops up right on schedule, and the camp lamp lights up warm on its own. The image: the camera sweeps across the brand logo printed on her backpack. She raises her hot coffee toward the camera, the backdrop a star-strewn snow-mountain pass — no couple elements at all, leaning into the counter-convention romance of solitude. End on the slogan: "A 5/20 promise still counts when it's made to the mountains."

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 运动鞋的品牌片。一个女孩独自在雪山营地扎营，「与山约会」定时提醒准时弹出，营地灯自动亮起暖光。画面表达：镜头扫过登山包上印的品牌logo。女孩对着镜头举了举手里的热咖啡，背景是缀满繁星的雪山垭口，没有任何情侣元素，主打反常规的独处浪漫。最后呈现slogan「520 的承诺，说给山听也作数」

## Luxury perfume

### A Mediterranean summer, awakened by scent

> A 15-second light-luxury perfume stream-of-consciousness visual short. The theme is the languid summer memory of the Mediterranean. The scene holds multiple Mediterranean motifs. Not a complete narrative — more like a memory awoken by a scent and then scattered by the wind, with a fragmented editing rhythm that fits the memory theme.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 一段 15 秒的轻奢香水意识流视觉短片。主题是地中海的慵懒夏日回忆。场景里有多个地中海意象。不要完整的叙事，而是像一阵被香气唤醒、又随风飘散的回忆，剪辑节奏破碎，与回忆主题契合。

## Automotive

### A future car

> This is not a car in the ordinary sense, but the arrival of a new order. It emerges from a calm, restrained, minimal space, carrying a near-silent sense of power, like the future arriving early into reality. The footage has no excess emotion — only a continuous forward sharpness and confidence, clean, precise, unshakable. What it represents is not speed itself, but a more advanced, more lucid, more aesthetically grounded way of living — like the outline of the next generation of urban civilization, slowly taking shape.

## Luxury jewelry

### "The light will speak for her"

> After a late-night banquet ends, a woman stays alone in the empty room. Her heels stop; her breathing is light; the jewelry at her ear and between her fingers occasionally flickers in the dim light — betraying her mood before her expression does. The whole film should have that cold, dangerous allure, like desire pressed very low but not gone. End on a single line: "The light will speak for her."

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 深夜宴会散场后，一个女人独自留在空荡的房间里。高跟鞋停下，呼吸很轻，耳边和指间的珠宝在昏暗光线里偶尔闪一下，比她的表情更先泄露情绪。整支片子要有那种冷艳又危险的吸引力，像欲望被压得很低，但没有消失。最后露出一句话："光会替她说。"

</details>
