# Cinematic narrative

Emotion-driven shorts, story fragments, micro-expressions, light & texture, and film pre-visualization — where "tell the story, trust the model" produces the most cinematic results.

## Emotion slices

### Loneliness

> An alien entity transmits some abstract signal to humankind. A woman culture-scholar in a minimal deep-space suit stands alone before the enormous signal, looking up at a giant gaseous sign suspended in a gray-blue sky. Her fingertip lightly touches the flowing gas as she softly says: "Does the universe still remember us?" No echo answers — only a low electromagnetic hum rolling across the empty planet. Countless fragments of a happy life on Earth flash by like a whirling lantern; then the image freezes. Her gaze is calm and desolate as she stands alone in the boundless interstellar wilderness, as if gently forgotten by the whole universe.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 外星生物向人类传达着某种抽象信号，穿着太空探测服的女文化学家独自站在庞大的信号前，身着简约深空防护服，仰头望向悬在灰蓝色天空的巨型气态信号，指尖轻轻触碰流动的气态信号，轻声说："Does the universe still remember us?"没有任何回音，只有低频电磁声在空旷星球回荡。无数在地球上快乐生活的片段如走马灯般快速闪回，骤然画面定格，她眼神平静又孤寂，独自伫立在无垠的星际荒野中，仿佛被整个宇宙轻轻遗忘。

</details>

### Suppressed grief

> A funeral in the pouring rain; everyone's black umbrellas merge into one mass. A little boy holds no umbrella, standing at the very outer edge of the crowd, the rain soaking his white shirt transparent. In his hand he clutches a small folded paper boat, staring at a puddle in the mud growing wider. Suddenly he crouches, sets the paper boat into the puddle, watches it float for two seconds — then a raindrop strikes it down.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 暴雨中的葬礼，所有人的黑伞连成一片。一个小男孩没有打伞，站在人群最外圈，雨水把他的白衬衫浇得透明。他手里攥着一只纸折的小船，盯着泥地上越来越大的水洼。忽然他蹲下来，把纸船放进水洼里，看着它漂了两秒，然后被雨滴砸沉了。

</details>

### Waiting & longing

> Ink-wash painting style. A mother stands beneath the old pagoda tree at the village entrance, gazing toward the distant mountain road, her sleeves lifted by the wind. The road is empty of people — only fallen yellow leaves carpeting the ground. She waits until the sky darkens, then turns to walk back, the road behind her vanishing bit by bit into the dusk.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 水墨画风格。一位母亲站在村口的老槐树下望向远方的山路，衣袂被风吹起。山路上空无一人，只有黄叶落了一地。她等到天色暗下来，转身往回走，身后的路在暮色中一点点消失。

</details>

## Micro-expressions

### A silent collapse on the last bus

> The very back row of an empty last bus; a middle-aged man in a black suit clutches a crumpled black-and-white photo. The city lights outside slide across his face one streak at a time. He lowers his head, wipes the face in the photo with his thumb, presses it to his chest, rests his forehead on the seatback in front, and his shoulders begin to tremble faintly. The bus reaches a stop, the lights come on — he doesn't move.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 空荡荡的末班公交最后一排，一个穿黑色西装的中年男人手里攥着一张皱巴巴的黑白照片。窗外城市的灯火一条一条滑过他的脸。他低头用拇指擦了擦照片上的脸，把照片贴在胸口，额头靠在前排椅背上，肩膀开始微微发抖。车到站了，灯亮了，他没有动。

</details>

### A small comfort

> A young man working very late eats a freshly-heated rice ball at a convenience-store doorway. He's exhausted — but on the first bite a tiny flicker of being-comforted crosses his face: an ordinary person's small moment in the city.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 一个加班很晚的年轻人，在便利店门口吃刚热好的饭团。他本来很疲惫，吃第一口时突然露出一点点被安慰到的表情，呈现城市里一个普通人的瞬间。

</details>

## Light & texture

### Before dawn at the fishing port

> Four a.m. at the fishing port, the sky not fully light. An old fisherman crouches at the bow twisting hemp rope, the knuckles of his fingers crowned with black calluses. Harbor lights filter through the mist, tracing his silhouette in a warm orange edge. The rope finished, he rises to look out to sea — the horizon splits open in a fish-belly white, and every mast turns to silhouette.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 凌晨四点的渔港，天没亮透。老渔民蹲在船头搓麻绳，手指关节鼓着黑色的茧。港口灯光从雾里透出来，把他的轮廓勾成一道暖橘色的边。绳子搓好，他起身望海 —— 海平线裂开一条鱼肚白，所有桅杆变成了剪影。

</details>

## Empty-shot transitions

### Snow on an empty bridge

> Eastern freehand-ink style. Heavy snow falls on a stone arch bridge with no one on it; the snow on the deck is thick enough to hide the seams between stones. The river below is ink-green, flowing slowly, carrying a few withered lotus leaves downstream. A crow lands on the railing, shakes its wings once, and a small clump of snow is shaken loose from the rail, falling soundlessly into the river.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 东方写意风格。大雪落在一座空无一人的石拱桥上，桥面的积雪已经厚到看不见石缝。桥下的河水是墨绿色的，缓慢流动，带着几片枯荷叶往下游漂。一只乌鸦落在桥栏上，抖了一下翅膀，桥栏上的雪被震落了一小撮，无声地坠入河面。

</details>

## Film pre-viz & big-scene blocking

### Burning chained ships (Red Cliff)

> The fire-attack on the chained ships at the Battle of Red Cliff. On the Yangtze, hundreds of Cao army warships are linked bow to stern, locked by iron chains. The instant the southeast wind rises, the first fire-arrow strikes the nearest sail — flames race up the canvas in a single second, then spread along the chains to the next ship. Within a few breaths the whole river surface ignites from a point into a line, then bursts from a line into an entire wall of fire. The firelight stains the river orange-red; thick smoke churns into a sky-blotting black mushroom cloud. Seen from high above and far off, the whole Yangtze is a burning dragon.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 三国赤壁之战的火烧连环船。长江江面上，曹军数百艘战船首尾相连，铁索横锁。东南风骤起的瞬间，第一只火箭射入最近的船帆——火焰沿着帆布窜上去只用了一秒，然后顺着铁索向下一艘船蔓延。短短几个呼吸之间，整个江面从一个点燃成一条线，再从一条线炸开成一整面火墙。火光把江水映成橙红色，浓烟在夜空中翻涌成遮天蔽日的黑色蘑菇云。从远处高空看下去，整条长江像一条正在燃烧的龙。

</details>

### Cyberpunk vs. medieval dark — side by side

> The same street in two versions, side by side: the left half is neon-flickering cyberpunk, flying cars streaking past, holographic ads projected into the rain; the right half is medieval-dark, flagstone roads, candlelight glowing through Gothic windows, cloaked figures walking through fog. Along the dividing line, the elements of the two worlds are bleeding into each other.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 同一条街道的两个版本并排呈现：左半边是霓虹闪烁的赛博朋克风格，飞行汽车掠过，全息广告投射在雨幕中；右半边是中世纪暗黑风格，石板路面，烛光从哥特窗户透出，穿斗篷的人影在雾中行走。分界线上两个世界的元素正在互相渗透。

</details>
