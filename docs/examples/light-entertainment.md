# Light entertainment

Absurd skits and healing animation — playful, high-concept ideas where a single vivid premise is enough.

## Absurd skits

### A historical mix-up

> At the center of a Roman Colosseum, a gladiator readies for battle. Walking toward him is not another gladiator, but a modern young man in a hoodie and headphones, carrying a takeout bag. The two make eye contact, both baffled. The Romans in the stands look equally confused. Cinematic comedy.

> 古罗马斗兽场中央，一个角斗士准备战斗。对面走来的不是另一个角斗士，而是一个穿着卫衣戴着耳机的现代年轻人，手里拿着外卖。两人对视，都很困惑。观众席的罗马人也一脸疑惑。电影感喜剧。

</details>

### Evolution of the office worker

> Monday morning. A suited office worker walks into the office, and with every cubicle he passes he ages a little — by the time he reaches his own seat he's a white-haired old man. He shakily opens his computer; the screen reads "Monday 9:01." Photorealistic comedy.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 周一早上，一个西装革履的上班族走进办公室，每经过一个工位他就衰老一点——走到自己座位时已经变成一个白发苍苍的老人。他颤巍巍地打开电脑，屏幕显示"周一 9:01"。超写实喜剧。

</details>

## Healing animation

### The runaway broccoli

> In a miniature kitchen made entirely of clay, a clay floret of broccoli suddenly becomes self-aware. It sees its companions being sliced up by a small silver knife, and bolts in terror. It launches a grand escape across the kitchen: using noodles as swinging vines, surfing the scalding frying pan like a skateboard, finally leaping into a fondue pot full of chocolate. When the chef scoops it out with a spoon, it shuts its eyes in despair — only to find that, coated all over in chocolate, it's been mistaken for a fancy dessert and has escaped the fate of being stir-fried.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 在一个完全由黏土捏成的微缩厨房里，一朵黏土西兰花突然有了意识。它看到同伴正在被一把银色的小刀切碎，吓得拔腿就跑……

</details>

### Van Gogh's *Starry Night*

> Van Gogh walks along a country path, but the whole world is in the style of *The Starry Night* — the stars in the sky swirl and flow, the cypress trees twist and sway like flame, the moonlight is heavy oil-paint brushstrokes. His own figure carries the oil-paint texture too, and where he walks the pigment trickles faintly. Post-Impressionist motion painting.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 梵高走在乡间小路上，但整个世界是《星月夜》的风格——天空的星星在旋转流动，柏树像火焰一样扭曲摆动，月光是厚重的油画笔触。他的身影也带着油彩质感，走过的地方颜料微微流淌。后印象派动态画。

</details>
