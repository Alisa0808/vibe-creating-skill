### Dotting the dragon's eyes

> An unconfident young painter is tasked with restoring a damaged ancient scroll. Everyone says his brushwork isn't strong enough — the dragon in the painting will never "come alive." He stays up all night completing the dragon's body, but can't bring himself to dot the eyes. On a stormy night, ink lit by lightning, he finally lays down the last stroke — the ink on the paper seamlessly transmutes into surging seas of cloud, and a great dragon soars up out of the painting. In the end the dragon doesn't fly away, but turns into a beam of golden light returning to his brush tip, telling him: "What truly brought me to life was your daring to put down the brush."

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 一个不自信的年轻画师接到任务，要修复一幅残缺古画。所有人都说他笔力不够，画中的龙永远"活不了"。他熬夜补完龙身，却迟迟不敢点眼睛。暴雨夜，墨水被雷光照亮，他终于落下最后一笔，画纸上的墨迹无缝质变成翻涌云海，巨龙从画中腾空而起。最后龙没有飞走，而是化作一道金光回到他的笔尖，告诉他："真正点亮我的，是你敢落笔。"

</details>

## Historical-era atmosphere

### Chang'an at the height of the Tang

> Dusk on Chang'an's Suzaku Avenue; a camel caravan of foreign merchants slowly passes through the city gate. The silks on the camels' backs sway in the wind; the banners of the roadside taverns are gilded by the setting sun. A Persian merchant hops down from a camel's back, slaps the dust off his boots, and gives the tavern owner a thumbs-up. Far off, flocks of returning birds hang on the eaves of the Great Wild Goose Pagoda. Cinematic texture, warm tones.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 黄昏的长安朱雀大街，一支胡商驼队正缓缓穿过城门。骆驼背上的丝绸随风摇摆，街边酒肆的幌子在落日中镀上金色。一个波斯商人从驼背上跳下来，拍了拍靴子上的尘土，对着酒肆老板竖起大拇指。远处大雁塔的檐角挂着归巢的鸟群。电影质感，暖色调。

</details>

### A Qin-dynasty imperial highway

> A perfectly straight rammed-earth road extends from beneath your feet to where it vanishes at the distant mountains. Every ten yards along both sides stands a green pine. A six-horse bronze chariot drives up from afar, its wheels raising fine dust as they roll over the road. The expressionless driver looks straight ahead; the wind lifts a corner of the cabin curtain, revealing stacked bamboo slips. The aerial shot rises slowly — the road cuts a line of ink across an entire expanse of green plain.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 一条笔直的夯土大道从脚下延伸到远山消失处。道路两侧每隔三丈立着一棵青松。一辆六马铜车从远处驶来，车轮碾过路面扬起细尘。驾车的御手面无表情目视前方，车厢帘子被风掀起一角，露出堆叠的竹简。航拍缓缓拉高，道路如一条墨线切开了整片苍绿的原野。

</details>

## Natural phenomena & science

### A supercell storm

> Over the central Great Plains of the USA, a giant supercell thunderstorm cloud rotates slowly, its base pressed very low and ink-green. At the center of the cloud base, a slowly-descending funnel-shaped protrusion appears, stretching toward the ground, its tip narrowing. The instant it touches down, dust and debris are violently sucked up, forming a grayish-brown rotating column connecting earth and sky. In the distance, the sheet-metal roof of a farmhouse is torn up and swept into the airflow. Documentary aerial perspective, real meteorological photography.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 美国中部大平原上，一团巨大的超级单体雷暴云正在缓慢旋转，云底压得很低，呈墨绿色。云底中央出现一个缓慢下垂的漏斗状突起，漏斗向地面伸长，尖端越来越细。当它触地的瞬间，地面的尘土和碎屑被猛烈吸起，形成一根灰褐色的旋转柱体连接天地。远处一座农舍的铁皮屋顶被撕起来卷入气流。纪录片航拍视角，真实气象摄影。

</details>

### Formation of a fertilized egg

> Under a microscope, in the dim warm environment of the fallopian tube, a swarm of long-tailed sperm swim hard toward a giant egg cell. The egg is like a brilliant planet, wrapped in a translucent jelly-like shell (the zona pellucida), giving off a soft warm-yellow glow. One sperm breaks through this barrier first, its head touching and successfully fusing with the egg membrane. At the moment of fusion, a ring of pale-blue glowing ripples spreads across the egg's surface (the cortical reaction), and the zona pellucida quickly hardens, repelling and blocking all the other sperm trying to enter. Medical-grade 3D educational animation style.

<details><summary>原文</summary>

> 显微镜头下，幽暗温暖的输卵管环境中，一群拖着长尾巴的精子正奋力游向一颗巨大的卵细胞。卵细胞像一颗璀璨的星球，外围包裹着一层半透明的果冻状外壳（透明带），散发着柔和的暖黄色微光。其中一颗精子率先突破这层屏障，头部与卵细胞膜触碰并成功融合。融合的瞬间，卵细胞表面立刻荡开一圈淡蓝色的发光涟漪（皮层反应），透明带随之迅速变硬，将其余试图进入的精子全部弹开阻挡在外。医疗级 3D 科普动画风格。

</details>
